Welcome to King Of Ads

Game Overview: King Of Ads is a simulation game where players take on the role of a budding ad executive. You'll balance resources, manage budgets, and make strategic decisions to create the best advertising campaigns. From selecting target audiences to allocating funds, every choice you make will impact your success, reputation, and growth within the competitive ad industry.

Gameplay Mechanics: Start with a modest budget and a limited resources, gradually increasing your skill, resources and team as you complete contracts successfully. You’ll encounter both high-reward contracts with ethical dilemmas and grassroots startups that offer unique reputational bonuses. Each contract you undertake adds to your company's reputation and opens up even bigger opportunities and understanding your town better.
